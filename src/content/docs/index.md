HappyHQ is the AI workspace for everyday work. You teach it how you do anything — just like you would teach a teammate — and it does that work for you, the way you would have done it.

It's open source. It runs on your machine. And everything you teach it is yours.

---

## AI you can teach

HappyHQ is built around a simple idea: you can teach AI how you do anything.

Take something you do routinely, or something you spend way too much time on. You teach HappyHQ how you want it done. What matters. What good looks like. HappyHQ learns everything it can about how you do this work — then it does the work for you.

For some folks that's a statement of work. For others it's user stories, meeting prep, client reports, weekly updates. Whatever your work is, if you can explain it, you can teach HappyHQ to do it.

This works even when your work is messy. Especially when your work is messy. All the "it depends" and "well for this client we do X" and "except on Fridays." HappyHQ handles it.

Instead of doing the work yourself, your time goes into managing the work. Reviewing it. Improving it. Teaching HappyHQ how to do it better. Then it keeps doing the work for you — every time, just the way you want it done.
